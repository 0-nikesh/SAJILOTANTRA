What exactly is SAJILOTANTRA?

SAJILOTANTRA is conceived to address the challenges citizens face in dealing with essential services, community involvement, and government procedures. It aims to simplify complex bureaucratic processes, provide a virtual space for community interaction, and assist users in locating government offices. Citizens often struggle with bureaucratic complexities, social isolation, and difficulties in accessing government services. SAJILOTANTRA intends to mitigate these challenges, encouraging a more connected, informed, and compassionate society.

Public Forum

We have a Community forum where users can share experiences, seek advice, and find solutions to common problems, fostering a sense of community and support for each others. Users can also like the posts to show their appreciation. Similarly, they can comment on the posts to share their opinion on any particular topic.

The posts made by any users is also collected in their profile page so they can check their activity on our application. We also have a delete feature if they want to delete any of their posts.
